Research

I am a masters student focussed on high-impact problems in healthcare and NLP using Deep Learning techniques, simultaneously advancing them. I love to work on hard problems in sequential data including Language and Time Series for unstructured or structured data. I have worked on many Problems in the past in these fields I am a very motivated person, passionate to learn and try to become better every day. I strive to produce results and persevere through all the way through. I have a strong interest in Machine Learning/Deep Learning, Data Science, Software Engineering and Quantum Computation. Currently, I'm a second-year Computer Science graduate student at the Texas A&M University. As a part of my research, I am currently working on developing novel Natural Language Understanding techniques using supervised and unsupervised techniques. Previous to that, I was working on Time Series Data in Heart Patients for the task of Summarization and Interpretability of Machine Learning and Deep Learning Risk Predictive models. The ability to embed intelligence into systems and artificially imitate human behavior, some of which even we are incapable of, is what excites me a lot about AI. I believe Deep learning will revolutionize intelligent machines.
